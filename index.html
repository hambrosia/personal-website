Hi. My name is Matt and I'm a graduate student in German Literature at UNC Chapel Hill and Duke University. I see language, literature and art as transformative media that force us to define who we are, how we relate to others and what we value. As a foreign language teacher, I aim to share this experience with my students and prepare them to meet today's increasingly complex personal and political challenges. I'm currently finishing a dissertation about German comics and graphic novels.
